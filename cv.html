Profile

AI researcher with a strong record across ML methods, vision, multimodal reasoning, education, and governance.

Most of my applied work has been in health AI because it is a demanding setting for modern ML: labels are noisy, rare cases matter, data are multimodal, evaluation is unforgiving, and deployment requires care. The underlying problems are broader than any one domain: learning from imperfect data, evaluating models honestly, and building systems people can understand and use.

ML methodsNoisy labels, sample difficulty, fairness, missingness, synthetic data
VisionMedical imaging, facial analysis, segmentation, classification, generative models
