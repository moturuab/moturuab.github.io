About

I work at the intersection of rigorous ML research and real-world health AI.

I am a University of Toronto Computer Science PhD candidate supervised by Dr. Anna Goldenberg and Dr. Babak Taati, with research spanning sample difficulty, noisy learning, fairness, medical imaging, automated pain monitoring, VLMs, and agentic AI.

I have also spent years teaching and translating AI for mixed technical and clinical audiences through the Data Sciences Institute and T-CAIREM. That combination—research depth, product awareness, and communication—is what I bring to high-impact AI teams.
