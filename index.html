About

A research-to-product AI profile built for high-stakes problems.

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate at the University of Toronto working with Dr. Anna Goldenberg and Dr. Babak Taati, with affiliations across the Vector Institute, SickKids, UHN KITE, T-CAIREM, and Massey College.

My strongest edge is combining rigorous ML research with practical health AI deployment: learning from noisy labels, estimating sample difficulty, improving fairness and generalization, working with radiologists and clinicians, and building educational and governance programs that help people use AI responsibly.

I am especially interested in roles where model reliability, product judgment, research depth, and clear communication all matter—applied AI research, research engineering, health AI, multimodal systems, and AI platform leadership.
