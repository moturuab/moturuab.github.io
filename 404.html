About

Research grounded in the conditions where models actually fail.

I study machine learning when labels are imperfect, examples differ in difficulty, and benchmark performance is not enough. That has led me from methods for sample weighting, label correction, and targeted synthetic data to clinical studies of AI-assisted imaging and automated pain assessment.

I also work on AI education and governance through T-CAIREM, and on agentic healthcare systems as CTO of CoordCare. Across these projects, the aim is the same: useful systems that remain legible, fair, and accountable in practice.
